# Vision Wealth Tracker

Vision Wealth Tracker is a personal finance management platform designed specifically for **salaried employees** to track their cash flow, manage financial ratios, monitor investments, and optimize wealth allocation.

The platform provides a **clear financial overview** by combining salary tracking, expense analysis, portfolio monitoring, and intelligent insights in a single dashboard.

---

# Problem

Many salaried individuals struggle with:

- Lack of visibility into where their salary goes
- Managing expenses vs savings effectively
- Tracking multiple investments across platforms
- Understanding financial health through meaningful metrics
- Maintaining disciplined wealth allocation

Most people use **multiple apps, spreadsheets, or manual calculations**, which leads to fragmented financial management.

---

# Solution

Vision Wealth Tracker solves this by providing a **single platform** where users can:

- Track monthly salary allocation
- Monitor expenses and investments
- Calculate financial health ratios
- Analyze portfolio performance
- Optimize savings and asset allocation

The platform helps users move from **passive saving to intentional wealth building**.

---

# Core Features

## Salary Allocation Tracking

Users can track how their salary is distributed across:

- Expenses
- Savings
- Investments
- Emergency funds

---

## Investment Portfolio Tracking

Track investments such as:

- Mutual Funds
- Stocks
- Fixed deposits
- Other assets

Users can monitor **investment growth and portfolio allocation**.

---

## Financial Ratio Analysis

The platform calculates important financial health indicators such as:

- Savings Ratio
- Investment Ratio
- Expense Ratio
- Emergency Fund Ratio

These ratios help users understand whether their finances are **balanced and sustainable**.

## Portfolio Insights

Analyze:

- Asset allocation

- Investment contribution patterns
